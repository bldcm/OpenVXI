VXIapResult GetOptimalStreamBufferSizeRange

(struct VXIapInterface* pThis,
  VXIulong* lowLimitBytes,
  VXIulong* highLimitBytes)

Retrieve the preferred range of audio buffer sizes for streaming
This retrieves the preferred audio buffer size range for streaming play operations, allowing clients to obtain a buffer size within that range whenever possible from streaming audio data sources such as text-to-speech engines. Note that this value is only a hint, the client will play audio with other buffer sizes in cases where the desired range cannot be satisfied by the audio source.

Parameters:
lowLimitBytes - [OUT] Lower limit of the optimal range, in bytes
highLimitBytes - [OUT] High limit of the optimal range, in bytes
Returns:
VXIap_RESULT_SUCCESS on success
