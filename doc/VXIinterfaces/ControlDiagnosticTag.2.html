Convenience function for enabling a diagnostic tag, identical to calling SBlogInterface::ControlDiagnosticTag

Parameters:
tagID - [IN] Identifier that classifies a group of logically associated diagnostic messages (usually from a single software module) that are desirable to enable or disable as a single unit. See the top of SBlog.h for tagID allocation rules.
state - [IN] Boolean flag to turn the tag on (TRUE) or off (FALSE).
Returns:
VXIlog_RESULT_SUCCESS on success
