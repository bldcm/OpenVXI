Open flags

Flags for Open( ), may be combined through bitwise or.
INET_FLAG_NULL
Null flag.
INET_FLAG_NONBLOCKING_IO
Non-blocking reads/writes.
The Open() call takes a bitwise or of open flags which control the behavior of the returned stream.
