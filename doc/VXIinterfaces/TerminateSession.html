VXIscResult TerminateSession

(struct VXIscInterface* pThis)

Terminate (hangup) the call
Ends the current session. The caller is responsible for also stopping the Audio Player and Audio Source interfaces if they are active.

Returns:
VXIsc_RESULT_SUCCESS on success
