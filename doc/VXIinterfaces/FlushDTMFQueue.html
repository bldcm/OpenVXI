VXIscResult FlushDTMFQueue

(struct VXIscInterface* pThis)

Remove all DTMF digits from the DTMF queue
Warnings: There is only one DTMF queue and it is only accessible via this routine and GetDTMF( ). In systems with multiple clients, they must negotiate among themselves for access to the queue.

Returns:
VXIsc_RESULT_SUCCESS on success
